Features

Everything you need to
trade futures smarter

NexFutures combines Claude AI with institutional-grade trading tools in a mobile-first package. Build, test, and deploy — right from your pocket.

⚙

AI-Powered Trading Bots

Build bots powered by Claude AI — choose from Haiku for speed, Sonnet for balance, or Opus for deep analysis. Combine AI insights with your own indicators and risk parameters.

⚡

Real-Time Market Data

Live quotes, depth of market, and streaming charts powered by direct WebSocket connections. No delays, no compromises.

📊

Custom Indicators

Build your own technical indicators or use the built-in library. Stack multiple signals to create powerful entry and exit conditions.

🛡

Risk Guards

Per-account max position limits and daily loss caps protect your capital. Automatic bracket orders with stop-loss and take-profit.

🔗

Tradovate Integration

Connect your Tradovate account with one tap via secure OAuth. Works with personal accounts and compatible prop firm evaluations.

📱

Mobile-First Design

Built natively in SwiftUI for iOS. Fast, responsive, and designed for traders who need to manage positions on the go.
